# Spotify Earnings Call & Release - Key Excerpts

## Most Recent: Q3 2025 (Reported November 4, 2025)

### CEO Daniel Ek Key Quotes
- "We're shipping faster than ever"
- "700 million users who keep coming back, engagement at all-time highs"
- Emphasised accelerated execution across music, podcasts, and audiobooks
- Expressed confidence in future profitability through pricing strategies

### Key Topics Discussed

#### 1. Profitability Momentum
- Operating income of €582M exceeded guidance by €97M
- Record free cash flow of €806M
- LTM free cash flow reached €2.9B
- Demonstrates sustained profitability (not a one-quarter event)

#### 2. Premium ARPU Pressure
- Q3 2025 Premium ARPU: €4.53 (down 4% YoY reported; flat in constant currency)
- ARPU gains from price increases are being offset by geographic and product mix shifts
- More subscribers coming from lower-ARPU markets
- This is a concern for investors: revenue per user declining even as total revenue grows

#### 3. Subscriber & User Growth
- 5M net Premium subscriber adds QoQ (276M -> 281M)
- 17M net MAU adds QoQ (696M -> 713M)
- Growth broad-based across geographies
- Engagement metrics at all-time highs

#### 4. Product Innovation & Diversification
- Audiobooks gaining traction as a revenue stream
- Video podcast capability expanding
- AI features being developed (led to Prompted Playlists in Dec 2025)

### Forward Guidance (for Q4 2025)
- MAUs: 745M
- Subscribers: 289M
- Revenue: €4.5B (+13% YoY)
- Gross Margin: 32.9%
- Operating Income: €620M
- Premium ARPU: ~2% YoY growth (constant currency)

## Q4 2024 Earnings (Reported February 4, 2025)

### Key Achievement: First Full Year of Profitability
- Full Year 2024 Operating Profit: €1.5B
- Full Year 2024 Free Cash Flow: €2.3B
- Q4 2024 Revenue: €4.2B (+16% YoY)
- Q4 2024 Gross Margin: 32.2% (+555 bps YoY)
- Q4 2024 Premium Subs: 263M (+11% YoY)
- Q4 2024 Free Cash Flow: €877M (+121% YoY)

### Management Commentary
- Marked a turning point - Spotify finally proving the business model works at scale
- Focus on operational efficiency and cost discipline
- Pricing power validated by minimal churn after 2024 price increases
