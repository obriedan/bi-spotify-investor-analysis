# Question 5 — Competitive Landscape

The 20-F describes competition as "robust, complex, and rapidly evolving." It explicitly names competitors across various verticals:

- **Music Streaming:** Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, Deezer, Joox, Pandora, and SoundCloud.
- **Podcasts:** Apple Podcasts, YouTube, Audible, Facebook, Pandora, Deezer, and TuneIn.
- **Audiobooks:** Amazon's Audible, Apple Books, Google Audiobooks, Librivox, Kobo Audiobooks, Downpour, Storytel, and BookBeat.
- **Advertising:** Alphabet, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft, Snap, Pinterest, iHeartMedia, and Pandora.
